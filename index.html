Admissions

For admission to LKG, parents will have to bring students and a non formal interview will be conducted minimum age requirements to LKG is 3 years and 6 months and maximum age is 4 years and 6 months.The age for admission to other classes will be regulated accordingly.Application form for admission to LKG and primary classes can be obtained from the school offcie on payement of RS 100 on all working days.Documents such as Transfer certificate from the previous school,original birth certificate, two photocopy of birth certificate to passport size photos are to be attached with the admission form.An Admission test for UKG and above will be conducted in the school and of the management decision will be final.
